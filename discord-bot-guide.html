Membership Verification

Before you can access private channels or use the tracker commands, you need to verify your Whop membership. This links your Discord account to your Soldi subscription.

!verify
Server Channel

Type this in any channel in the Soldi Discord server. The bot will send you a DM asking for the email address you used on Whop. Reply with your email and the bot checks your membership status. If you have an active subscription, you'll automatically get the Soldi Paid Member role which unlocks all private channels.

!verify
→ Bot DMs you: "Please reply with the email address you used to purchase your Whop membership."
→ You reply: your@email.com
→ Bot: "✅ Verified! You now have the Soldi Paid Member role."

Troubleshooting

  • Make sure your DMs are open for server members. Go to Discord Settings → Privacy & Safety → Allow direct messages from server members.
  • Use the exact email you signed up with on Whop.
  • Your verification link expires after 30 minutes. If it times out, just type !verify again.

Polymarket Whale Tracker

The whale tracker is one of the most powerful tools in Soldi. It monitors Polymarket wallets in real time and sends you a DM every time a tracked wallet places a bet. You can track up to 50 custom wallets on top of the default whale list we already monitor for you.

All tracker commands are sent via DM to the Soldi bot. Just click on the bot's name and message it directly.

!track 0x...
DMs Only

Add a Polymarket wallet address to your personal tracking list. You can track up to 50 addresses. The bot will automatically look up the trader's name on Polymarket if they have a public profile. Once tracked, you'll get a DM alert every time that wallet places a bet — including the market, position, amount, and odds.

!track 0xac44cb78be973ec7d91b69678c4bdfa7009afbd7
→ Bot: "✅ Now tracking TheWhaleKing — You'll receive DM alerts when this address places a bet."
!untrack 0x...
DMs Only

Remove a wallet from your tracking list. Use the same address you used when you added it.

!untrack 0xac44cb78be973ec7d91b69678c4bdfa7009afbd7
→ Bot: "Stopped tracking 0xac44cb...fbd7"
!mytrackers
DMs Only

See all the wallets you're currently tracking. Shows each address with a number label and tells you which ones are also in the default whale list. Also shows how many of your 50 slots you've used.

!mytrackers
→ 1. 0xac44cb...fbd7 (default whale)
→ 2. 0x6a72f6...3ee
→ 3/50 slots used
!whales
DMs Only

View the full list of default whale wallets that Soldi tracks automatically. These are the biggest and most profitable Polymarket traders. Their bets are posted in the #polymarket-tracker-bot channel and you can also get DM alerts by adding them to your personal list.

!whales
→ Default Whale Addresses (38 wallets tracked)
!pnl
DMs Only

View the all-time profit and loss for every wallet you're tracking plus all default whales. The bot fetches live data from Polymarket and shows each wallet's total profit or loss sorted from most to least profitable. This is great for figuring out which whales are worth following.

!pnl
→ Fetching PnL for 42 wallets...
→ 1. TheWhaleKing — +$1,245,320
→ 2. 0xd25c72...34d20 — +$892,100
→ ...

How to Find Wallet Addresses

Go to polymarket.com/leaderboard and click on any trader's profile. Their wallet address is in the URL or on their profile page. Copy the full 0x... address and use it with the !track command.

System Status

Check if everything is running smoothly at any time.

!status
Anywhere

Runs a health check on all Soldi services and reports back with the status of each one. Shows uptime for the website server, the odds engine (including which sportsbooks and sports are active), the Discord bot, the Polymarket tracker, and the Twitter/X bot. If something's down, you'll see it here.

!status
→ ✅ Website Server — Online, uptime: 14d 6h
→ ✅ SoldiAPI (Odds Engine) — 12 scrapers, 5 sports
→ ✅ Discord Bot — Online, uptime: 14d 6h
→ ✅ Polymarket Tracker — 8 users tracked
→ ✅ Twitter/X Bot — last activity 12m ago

Tips & Best Practices

Get the Most Out of the Bot

  • DM the bot for tracker commands. The tracking commands (!track, !untrack, !mytrackers, !whales, !pnl) only work in DMs to keep the server channels clean.
  • Check !pnl before tracking new wallets. Use the profit/loss data to find the most profitable whales and focus your tracking on them.
  • Watch the #polymarket-tracker-bot channel. Default whale bets are posted there automatically so you don't miss anything even without DM alerts.
  • Track wallets from the Polymarket leaderboard. The top traders on the leaderboard are consistently profitable — add their addresses and follow their moves.
  • You have 50 slots. That's a lot of wallets. Start with the defaults and add anyone you spot making big plays.
