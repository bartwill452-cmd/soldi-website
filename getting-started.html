Welcome to Soldi! Here's how to get started.

Everything you need to start making money online, all in one place.

1

Log Into Your Dashboard

Head over to trysoldi.com and click Login in the top right. Enter the email you used when you purchased your membership. You'll get a verification code sent to your inbox, punch that in and you're good to go. This is your home base for everything, not Whop. Bookmark trysoldi.com because that's where all your tools and guides live.

2

Your Dashboard

Once you're logged in you'll see your member dashboard. You've got your API key which you'll need for the Odds Screen, your affiliate link where you earn 25% on every person you refer, written guides covering sports betting, e-commerce, AI selling, and affiliate programs, the AI Chatbot Builder where you can build bots and sell them to businesses, and the Google Review Campaign tool.

3

Join the Discord

Click the Discord link on your dashboard or head to discord.gg/HStNMpCAH5 to join the community. Once you're in the server type !verify in any channel. The bot will send you a DM asking for your email. Reply with the email you used on Whop and you'll automatically get the Soldi Paid Member role. This unlocks all the private channels, strategy calls, and live picks.

4

Setting Up the Polymarket Whale Tracker

This is one of the most powerful tools we have. In Discord type !whales to see the default whale wallets we're already tracking for you. If you want to add your own, type !track followed by the wallet address. You'll get a DM alert every time a tracked wallet places a bet. Type !mytrackers to see everything you're watching and !pnl to check profit and loss stats across all tracked wallets.

5

The Odds Screen

From your dashboard click Open Odds Screen and enter your API key when it asks. This gives you real time odds across 10+ sportsbooks including FanDuel, DraftKings, Pinnacle, BetRivers, Caesars, BetOnline, Bookmaker, and more. You can switch between NBA, NFL, MLB, NHL, and MMA. Look for the highlighted edges where one book has way better odds than the rest.

6

Read the Guides

We've put together detailed guides on everything you need. The betting guides walk you through sportsbook setup, unit sizing, priming, and using the devig tool. The AI websites guide shows you how to find leads, price your services, build sites, and scale. The ecom guide is a full 12 step blueprint from product research to Facebook ads. And the affiliate guide covers bank signup bonuses, cashback platforms, and crypto exchange offers.

7

Build and Sell AI Chatbots

Head to the Chatbot Builder on your dashboard. Set up a business name, add the services and FAQs, pick a tone, and you'll get an embeddable widget you can install on any website. Members are selling these to local businesses for $500 to $2,000 upfront plus a monthly retainer fee for ongoing maintenance and updates.

Important

Your Whop account is just for billing and managing your subscription. Everything else happens at trysoldi.com. All your tools, guides, the odds screen, chatbot builder, and community access are there. If you ever need help type !commands in Discord for a full walkthrough of the bot or submit a bug report from your dashboard.
